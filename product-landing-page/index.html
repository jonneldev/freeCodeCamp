Legendary Swords of Onepiece

Discover the finest collection of swords inspired by the legendary craftsmen of Wano Country.

Ame no Habakiri

Ame no Habakiri, also known as "Snake Slayer," is a sword wielded by Kozuki Oden. Crafted from the toughest materials found in Wano, it has the unique ability to harm even the mythical beast, Yamata no Orochi.

Shusui

Shusui, once wielded by the legendary samurai Ryuma, is a cursed sword with unmatched cutting power. Forged using ancient techniques by master swordsmiths, it is said to thirst for the blood of its enemies.

Enma

Forged by the legendary swordsmith Oden, Enma is known for its ability to channel and cut through immense power. It is said that its blade can slice through anything, even the toughest armor.
